Lecture on Corporate and Commercial Laws

Event Overview: The VMLS Research Committee recently hosted an insightful guest lecture titled “The Art of Research in Corporate and Commercial Laws,” featuring Ms. Utkarsha Nikam. Ms. Nikam is a seasoned legal professional and In-house Legal Counsel (U.K.) with extensive experience in corporate law.

VMLS | 20 minutes ago | 1 min read

Key Highlights

Speaker: Ms. Utkarsha Nikam

Topic: The Art of Research in Corporate and Commercial Laws

Audience: Law students at Vinayaka Mission’s Law School (VMLS)

Lecture Summary: Ms. Utkarsha Nikam delivered a comprehensive lecture focusing on the practical aspects of conducting research in corporate laws. She provided valuable insights into the workings of law firms and the significance of various sources for research in commercial law topics.

Major Points Discussed:

  • Interdisciplinary Nature of Research: Ms. Nikam highlighted how corporate and commercial law research often requires an interdisciplinary approach, integrating various sources and perspectives.
  • Importance of Thorough Research: Emphasizing the need for meticulous research, she explained how it aids in understanding complex legal issues and is essential for preparing for a successful career in corporate law.
  • Practical Examples and Case Studies: The lecture was enriched with practical examples and case studies, which the students found particularly enlightening. These examples helped illustrate the real-world application of legal research.
  • Career Guidance: Ms. Nikam also shared tips on building a successful career in corporate law, stressing the importance of developing strong research skills.

Student Reception: The lecture was well-received by the students. They found Ms. Nikam’s examples and case studies particularly enlightening and appreciated the practical knowledge and insights she provided. Conclusion: The guest lecture by Ms. Utkarsha Nikam provided an invaluable opportunity for students at VMLS to gain practical knowledge and insights into corporate and commercial law research. This event has significantly enriched the students’ academic experience and has equipped them with essential skills for their future careers.
