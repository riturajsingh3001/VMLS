4th Guest Lecture CPIL

The Centre for the Promotion of International Law (CPIL) at VMLS successfully hosted its 4th guest lecture on the theme 'Teaching and Researching International Law in Asia: Critical Approaches, and Other Research Methodologies' on 03rd June 2024. The lecture was conducted online and featured Mr. Amiel Ian Valdez, a distinguished Research Fellow at the Centre for International Law, National University of Singapore, and the Asian Society of International Law.

VMLS | 03rd June, 2024 | 1 min read

Highlights of the Event

The event began with a welcome address by the Director of the Centre for the Promotion of International Law. He introduced Mr. Amiel Ian Valdez and highlighted his extensive experience and contributions to the field of international law, particularly in the Asian context.

Mr. Valdez delivered an insightful lecture that covered the following key points:

  • The relevance and evolution of international law in Asia.
  • Critical approaches to teaching and researching international law.
  • Methodologies and best practices for conducting research in international law.
  • The role of educators and researchers in shaping the discourse on international law in Asia.

Following the lecture, an interactive Q&A session was held where participants posed questions to Mr. Valdez. The discussion provided deeper insights into specific research methodologies and the challenges faced in the field of international law in Asia.

The event concluded with a vote of thanks by Ms. Nabeela Siddiqui, expressing gratitude to Mr. Valdez for his enlightening lecture and to all participants for their active engagement.
