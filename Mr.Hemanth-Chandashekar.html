Mr. Hemanth Chandrashekar

Manager - People, Partnerships. & Career Support

With a career spanning over two decades, Hemanth Chandrashekar brings a wealth of expertise in Talent Acquisition, Business Development, Marketing, Sales, Strategic Alliances, Account Management, and Client Servicing within the realms of Technology (Products/Services) and the Recruitment industry.

Hemanth’s experience encompasses various dimensions, including Campus and Off-Campus recruitment, as well as Vertical and Volume hiring. He has demonstrated his proficiency in the Technology and Management sector by collaborating with distinguished companies such as HCL, ACS, NESS, Covansys, and CSC, catering to both the Indian and USA markets.

Hemanth has a proactive leadership style and a knack for building things from the ground up. He’s especially passionate about EdTech startups and has a talent for creating supportive environments for new entrepreneurs. He holds a Bachelor of Science degree, specializing in Computer Science, earned from St. Joseph’s Arts and Science College in Bangalore.
